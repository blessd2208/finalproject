At Eco Cleaners Solution we strive to make the world a cleaner and more environmentally greener place. Our commercial cleaning program follows eco-conscious practices and offers eco-friendly cleaning and disinfection for any business or industry.

We shall disinfect your premises using Electrostatic Application Technology. Electrostatic Application is a disinfection preventative process using state of the art electrostatic technology to apply disinfectant chemistry. Electrostatic Spray Technology uses an electrical charge to allow appropriate sanitizers and disinfectants to wrap around and evenly coat all types of surfaces for a more complete sanitation process than simple hand wiping or other standard means of disinfecting surfaces.
